Spicy Pineapple Margarita

Description

This spicy pineapple margarita brings some jalapeno and chili-lime seasoning to your tropical margarita! Use as little or as much as you like.

Ingredients

  1. 1 lime wedge
  2. 1 teaspoon chili-lime seasoning (such as Tajin)®
  3. 2 cup ice, or as needed
  4. 2 fluid ounces silver tequila
  5. 3 jalapeno slices, or more to taste
  6. 4 fluid ounces pineapple juice
  7. 3/4 fluid ounces triple sec or other orange liqueur
  8. 3/4 fluid ounces lime juice

Steps

  1. Springle chili lime seasoning onto a plate. Moisten the rim of a glass with lime wedge. Press the moistened rim into seasoning. Fill the glass with ice.
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with tequila and 2 jalapeon slices. Use a muddler to crush the peppers. Add pineapple juice, triple sec, and lime juice to the shaker and add 1 cup of ice. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds
  3. Strain margarita into the glass and garnish with remaining jalapeno slice, if desired.
