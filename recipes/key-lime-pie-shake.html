Key Lime Pie Shake Recipe

Description

This scrumptious Kely lime pie shake has all the elements of the famous pie. It sure is a tasy treat on a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  1. 1/3 cup vanilla ice cream
  2. 1/4 cup 1% milke
  3. 2 tablesppons key lime juice
  4. 2 squares graham crackers, divided
  5. 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  6. 2 tablespoons whipped cream
  7. 1/4 zest and 1 wedge from 1 key lime

Steps

  1. Add ice cream, milk, key lime juice, 1 graham cracker square, and sweetened condensed milk to the jar of a blender. Blend on high until smooth

  2. Pour milkshake into a glass. Top with whipped cream and zest. To garnish, break remaining graham square in half, and place one graham into whipped cream, then crumble remaining graham over milkshake. Cut a lime wedge and tuck into whipped cream
